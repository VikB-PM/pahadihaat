Privacy Policy & Data Storage

How We Store Your Data

At PahadiHaat, we use Google Forms to collect and store your information securely. Here's what you need to know:

Data Collection
  • We only collect information necessary to create your seller account
  • Your data is stored in a secure Google Sheet
  • Only authorized PahadiHaat team members can access your information
Data Usage
  • We use your information to contact you about your seller account
  • We never sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • You can request to delete your information at any time
Next Steps

After you submit the registration form, our team will contact you within 24 hours to complete your seller account setup and explain how to list your products.

Note: We're currently using Google Forms for data collection as we build our platform. In the future, we'll implement a more advanced system with Azure databases.
